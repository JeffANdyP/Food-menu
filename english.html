About us

The Art of Tamales, An Authentic Culinary Experience
en NYC

At Rosario's Food, we are dedicated to sharing our passion for Mexican and Guatemalan tamales with the world. We are lovers of gastronomy and we firmly believe in preserving the culinary traditions that have been bequeathed to us over generations. Our goal is to offer a unique and authentic experience, where tamales become in a work of culinary art.
